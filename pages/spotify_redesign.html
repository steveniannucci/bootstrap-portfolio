Spotify Mobile App Playback Feature Redesign


Previous Next

Description

The Spotify playback feature is designed for media playback of the songs available to listen to on the platform. In this redesign, I kept a lot of the same parts but changed and simplified the layout on the mobile version. The title of the song and the artist is shown on the screen. The play/pause button can be pressed to toggle between pausing a song (first slide) or playing it (second slide). The icon on the button will update from the toggle. Pressing the skip icons next to the button will manually change the song. The left icon is blurred out when the first song in the queue is playing. Pressing it will change the song to the last one that played. Pressing the right icon will change the song to the next one in the queue. The bar is draggable where the user can change which part of the song is playing. The timestamp on the left will update depending on how far into the song the bar is. Pressing the add button, respresenting the heart icon, will add the song to the liked songs playlist (third slide). Pressing the minus or dislike button on the right will instantly skip the song and remove it from the suggested songs (fourth slide).
